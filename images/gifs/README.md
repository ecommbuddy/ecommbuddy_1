# Service GIFs

Place your service GIF files here with the following naming convention:

- `service-1.gif` - Website Development
- `service-2.gif` - CRO Optimisation
- `service-3.gif` - SEO Optimisation
- `service-4.gif` - Growth Marketing
- `service-5.gif` - Social Media Marketing & Management
- `service-6.gif` - Ad Creation & Copywriting
- `service-7.gif` - Content Creation (Graphics & Video)

The GIFs will automatically display in the Services section with alternating left/right positioning.

**Note:** Until you add your GIFs, placeholder images will be shown automatically.
